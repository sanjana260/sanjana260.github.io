Capstone Research Project – Mergers and Acquisitions in India

Conducted data analysis on data related to mergers and acquisitions in India, identifying general patterns in the merger landscape utilizing econometrics and statistical techniques. We evaluated the economic impact of mergers on company stock prices using event study methodology, conducted ratio analysis, and performed regression and granger causality to quantify the influence of macroeconomic variables on merger activity in the country. We authored a research paper detailing our findings and presented it to a panel of professors and industry professionals, showcasing strong analytical and communication skills.
